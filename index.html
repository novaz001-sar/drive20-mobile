Driving Gestures

  • Swipe up on any empty game area to move forward.
  • Swipe left or right to turn.
  • Swipe down to move backward.
  • Tap Menu to pause, select a level, or reset.

Map and Editor

  • Before driving, review the route on the large map.
  • In the editor, choose Wall, Path, Start, or Goal, then drag across the grid to draw.
  • After saving a custom map, play it from Custom Mode.
